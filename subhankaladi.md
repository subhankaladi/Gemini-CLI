# Subhan Kaladi

Subhan Kaladi is a frontend and full-stack developer with expertise in technologies such as React, Next.js, and TypeScript. He focuses on creating type-safe, responsive, and interactive web applications, and also builds scalable full-stack applications.

Beyond web development, Kaladi specializes in AI and automation, developing intelligent systems with Agentic AI, and implementing advanced algorithms for forex trading analysis and trading systems. His educational background includes advanced studies in TypeScript, Next.js, Python, and Agentic AI, with a particular focus on the OpenAI Agents SDK. He has also completed Python programming fundamentals and is involved in learning Agentic AI fundamentals, LangChain, and multi-agent systems through the PIAIC - Artificial Intelligence Program.

Subhan Kaladi is an active content creator, sharing his knowledge on his YouTube channel, where he covers topics like full-stack deployment, prompt engineering, and AI. He also contributes articles on web development and Python to Medium. His work and projects, including those related to OpenAI Agents SDK and his academic pursuits, are available on GitHub.
